Real Estate Services

With real estate services,you hire a single firm to handle both your design and construction.Conflicts between the architect and contractor are eliminated, making the process easier and more enjoyable for you. Our architects and interior designers sit next to our estimators so there is a constant dialog on how to design your project in the most cost-efficient way.

You deserve to work with a firm reliable enough to protect your investment as their own. Only Wolves, a Nigeria based construction & architectural firm can skillfully address your discerning needs. We make sure to deliver affordable and magnificient buildings with quality materials
